Creating Community for King Pie in a Covid-19 year

King Pie

As South Africa went into Covid-19 lockdown, the social team focussed on creating engaging content to let Mzansi know that King Pie was in lockdown with them, keeping their fans engaged with the world of King Pie. During the year, we grew the brand’s following by 47 884, reaching over 38 million South Africans.

Par the car ... we're driving the leads

Admyt

The social team set out to create a lead generation campaign for admyt, a smart and ticketless parking payment facility found in a variety of malls in Johannesburg. With the aim of driving website sign-ups over a 4 week period, we successfully achieved our goals with 1 362 900 impressions, a reach of 430 326 and over 16 000 clicks.
