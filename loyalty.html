The more you purchase the more you earn

Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola was facing a challenge with the 1.35L and 1.5L glass bottle in the BOP environment and needed to regain share. We developed a reward platform that allowed the consumer to have variety in terms of their instant gratification and catered to their need state. We rewarded consumers for their purchases, the more they purchased the more they won. 40 million entries later, we restored market share and an overall positive ROI was seen.
