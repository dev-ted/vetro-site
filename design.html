Our work

Adding personality to flavour

Tic Tac

Tic Tac wanted to add depth to their four flavour profiles. By creating four personas, one for each flavour, each with their own charms, characteristics and pieces of iconography, we successfully created an interactive brand experience for online audiences.

The art of animation in creating an immersive brand experience

King Pie

As lead agency for King Pie, we had the opportunity to bring one of the brand’s digital competitions to life through the animated, spy-enthralled adventures of the King Pie man in the SPIE Adventures. This project really set the bar for how we transformed social experiences for followers via animation on the brand’s various social platforms.

Turning ordinary in the extraordinary

Cotlands

We had a lot of fun helping Cotlands, a non-profit that focuses on early childhood development and the power of play, with their TVC. The campaign followed the lines of the traditional hero concept, where the protagonist of the story transitioned from an ordinary life to an extraordinary one.
